ABOUT US

We are a team of dedicated people passionate about creating custom video content. We are committed to delivering the best results for our clients by being collaborative and focused on helping you achieve your objectives

Our job is to be your partner. As a partner we strive to see things from your perspective then use all of our experience to produce content that moves your audience to act. We know the days of posted one video and leaving it out there forever are gone. In order for your content to be effective you have to consistently publish fresh content for your audience. The packages we’ve designed are for the express purpose of having a campaign versus one off and random videos.

OUR PROCESS

Creative Consultation

We meet to discuss video strategy, key messages and identify people /places/activities to appear in the video.

Video Blueprint

We organize thoughts and ideas and
lay out a plan for the video. 

Scheduling & Shooting

We shoot on the scheduled date and gather additional footage on location. 

Client Review & Delivery

An initial draft is provided of the video for feedback and revisions.  When revisions are complete, the video goes live.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTONS

I don't want to be on camera, can we just use images?

There are a different options available. We can incorporate a music background track, just use your voice, use a voice over artist or any combination of these options.

I want to have a video created but, I still need to run my business. How do we work around my schedule?

We'll do just that, work around your schedule.

How long will the process take?

That varies depending on the complexity of the production. We will discuss that with you once we determine the project scope

How long will the completed video be?

Lengths vary depending on your goals, type of video and what's appropriate for your audience. That will be discussed and decided in our initial meeting.
